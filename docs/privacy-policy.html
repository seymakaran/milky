PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 09/08/2025

As ODEgames, we highly prioritize the privacy of children and parents. This Privacy Policy applies to our applications on both Android (Google Play) and iOS (App Store).

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Information

Our applications do not collect personal information such as email, phone number, name, or location from children or parents. No account or sign-in is required.

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, anonymous and non-personal data to improve app performance and deliver ads:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Basic usage statistics (e.g., which games or sections are used more frequently)
  • Crash/diagnostic reports (to resolve technical issues)

2. Third-Party Services

2.1. Advertising

Our apps include third-party advertisements provided by trusted ad networks that comply with child-privacy policies (e.g., AdMob, Unity Ads). These networks may use anonymous identifiers for ad delivery, frequency capping, and fraud prevention.

After a successful in-app purchase (Unlock All Games), all advertisements are permanently removed.

Ad network privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads Privacy Policy

2.2. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics (e.g., Firebase, Unity Analytics) to collect only anonymous and aggregated usage data to improve app performance and fix errors. No personal or identifiable information is collected.
