PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 3/1/2025

As ODEgames, we highly prioritize the privacy of children and parents. This privacy policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects data.

1. Collected Information

1.1. Personal Information

Our application does not collect personal information from children or parents.

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

To improve user experience, our application may collect the following anonymous information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Usage statistics (e.g., which games or sections are played more frequently)
  • Anonymous data through cookies or similar technologies

2. Use of Information

We may use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Enhancing the functionality of the application
  • Improving user experience
  • Resolving technical issues
  • Communicating with parents (if an email address is provided)

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share children's personal information with third parties. However, data may be shared in the following cases:

  • If required by legal obligations
  • With trusted service providers for application maintenance (only anonymous data)

4. Third-Party Links

Our application does not contain third-party advertisements to ensure child safety. However, there may be external links or purchase options directed at parents.

5. Parental Rights

As a parent, if we collect your child's personal information, you have the right to:

  • Review the information
  • Request its deletion
  • Refuse data collection

For such requests, please contact us at support@odegames.com.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect children's data. However, please note that data transmission over the internet cannot be 100% secure.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Parents will be notified of any updates, or automatically will be updated.
